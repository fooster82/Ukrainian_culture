Ukrainian Culture

Ukranian Sport

Football is the most popular sport in Ukraine. The top professional league is the Vyscha Liha, also known as the Ukrainian Premier League. The two most successful teams in the Vyscha Liha are rivals FC Dynamo Kyiv and FC Shakhtar Donetsk.

Although Shakhtar is the reigning champion of the Vyscha Liha, Dynamo Kyiv has been much more successful historically, winning the UEFA Cup Winners' Cup two times and the UEFA Super Cup once

Elina Mykhailivna Svitolina is a Ukrainian professional tennis player. She reached her career-high ranking in singles of world No. 3 on 11 September 2017

After the fall of the Soviet Union, a handful of former Soviet gymnasts started competing for the Ukraine. They participated as a team in the 1996, 2000, and 2004 Olympic Games. In 1999, they won their only World Championships team medal, a bronze.
